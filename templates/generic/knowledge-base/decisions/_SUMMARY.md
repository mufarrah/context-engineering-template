## Overview

Architecture Decision Records documenting WHY key technical decisions were made. These provide historical context and rationale for significant choices. Each decision file follows the standard ADR format: Context, Options Considered, Decision, and Consequences.

## ADR Format

Each decision follows the standard ADR format:
- **Context**: What prompted the decision
- **Options Considered**: Alternatives evaluated
- **Decision**: What was chosen
- **Consequences**: Trade-offs and impacts
